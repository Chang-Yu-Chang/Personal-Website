About me

  • I am broadly interested in combining experiments and computational approaches to study biological phenomena.

  • In my PhD study, under the supervision of Alvaro Sanchez and other amazing people in the lab, I am working on how to predicting microbial community assembly. Here are the research projects I am working on.

  • Besides research, I am very into the idea of data analysis and code reproducibility using R markdown. For example, I enjoyed learning how to make this website using blogdown and data-driven CV using pagedown.
